Privacy Policy

App: Muslims Fortress

Publisher: Lapidary

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Overview

Muslims Fortress is an Islamic companion app that provides prayer times, Qibla direction, Islamic calendar, duas/azkar, favorites, and reminders.

2. Data We Process

Location data

If you grant location permission, the app reads your device location (precise or approximate) to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction.

If location permission is denied, the app may use IP-based geolocation to estimate location.

App settings and user content (stored on device)

The app stores preferences and feature settings on your device, including theme, language, notification settings, manual location, favorites, and tasbih values.

App activity and diagnostics

The app may keep local cache and local logs for feature reliability (for example cached prayer times and local state). We do not run a first-party analytics or ads SDK in the app.

3. Network Requests and Third Parties

The app can send limited data to these services to provide features:

  • AlAdhan API (`api.aladhan.com`) for prayer times and Hijri/Gregorian conversions.
  • IP geolocation providers (`ipapi.co`, `ip-api.com`) as fallback when direct location is unavailable.
  • Platform geocoding providers through Android Geocoder (provider depends on device/OS).

Requests may include coordinates, date, timezone, and device IP address as part of normal web requests. Third-party processing is governed by each provider's own privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with advertising networks. Data may be transmitted to the third-party services listed above to fulfill requested app features.

5. Data Retention

Most app data is stored locally on your device. You can clear app data or uninstall the app at any time.

6. Security

Sensitive manual location values are stored using encrypted preferences when supported by your device. Network traffic is configured for HTTPS.

7. Children

Muslims Fortress is intended for general audiences and is not directed specifically to children.

8. Your Choices

  • Disable location permission in Android settings.
  • Disable notifications in app or system settings.
  • Use manual location mode instead of automatic location.
