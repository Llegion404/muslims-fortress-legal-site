FAQ

What does the app need location for?

To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction accurately.

Does the app work without location permission?

Yes. It can use manual location or approximate fallback methods.

Does the app send any data to the internet?

Yes, for specific features. The app may call prayer/calendar APIs and IP geolocation APIs. It also may use platform geocoding services.

Does the app use analytics or ads SDKs?

Not in the current Android codebase.

What data is kept on device?

Settings, favorites, tasbih state, and prayer/location cache.

How do I remove my data?

Clear app storage in Android settings or uninstall the app.
